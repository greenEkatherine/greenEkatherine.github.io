Description

Bilingual crosswords are an easy and fun way to learn new foreign words. Currently, we suggest only the English-Russian version. There are many topics to choose from and the ability to generate crosswords countless times. On the first launch, select your level of English to adjust words provided for crosswords. Then you may change the level anytime in the settings. Correct answers are highlighted immediately and give you quick feedback. If you don't know an answer, you may use hints for one letter or the whole word. Those hints cost some number of stars â€” solve crosswords to earn more of them. Just try, solve, train your memory and improve your vocabulary!

You will find:

  • different topics to generate crosswords (either single or multiple)
  • different language levels
  • generated crosswords are not limited
  • portrait screen mode
  • hints for letters and words
  • offline mode
  • no advertising
